Immerse yourself in traditional Japanese culture with a visit to Meiji Shrine, a significant Shinto shrine. Witness traditional Japanese rituals and deity offerings. Head to the Imperial Palace East Garden.

Continue to Asakusa, Tokyo's old town. Here, enter the massive gates to the ancient Buddhist temple of Senso-ji, which is Tokyo's oldest temple and wander down Nakamise shopping street that for centuries has been providing temple visitors with many traditional local snacks and souvenirs.

Afterward, head to Odaiba Seaside Park, one of the most popular tourist destinations.

breakfast , lunch

Take Shinkansen from Tokyo to Kyoto. Enjoy your day in Kyoto city.

Head to Kiyomizudera temple, perched on a hillside with sweeping views of Kyoto. Walk through its iconic wooden stage and feel the serenity of this UNESCO World Heritage Site. Dive into the heart of Kyoto at Nishiki Market, a bustling food haven. Savor local delicacies, from fresh seafood to matcha treats, and soak in the vibrant atmosphere

Explore the mesmerizing Fushimi Inari Shrine, famous for its thousands of red torii gates.

End your day in Gion, Kyoto’s historic geisha district. Stroll along its charming streets, spot traditional tea houses, and feel the timeless elegance of old Japan.

Osaka

breakfast , lunch

Head to Osaka castle, a historic landmark surrounded by lush gardens.

Next, set sail on the Santa Maria Cruise, a replica of Columbus’s ship, and enjoy stunning views of Osaka Bay and the city skyline from the water.
